To create the perfect blend:

Essential oil blends should be used with a carrier oil. We recommend argan oil, but you can also use coconut oil.
The vertical bar shows the amount of drops and the horizontal bar shows which essential oil should be used.
Mix the amount of drops in 10ml of your chosen carrier oil and use it in a diffuser or in a spray bottle.
